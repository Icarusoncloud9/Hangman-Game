Guess the International Territory

Rules of the game: The game is hangman. You have a defined amount of chances to guess the right letters of a country, island, or geographical location generated randomly. Each time you type a letter in, it will count the amount of letters you have guessed correctly, incorrectly, and whether you have won or lost the game in a counter located on the side.

When you have understood the rules of the game, you can press the button at the bottom of the screen to begin the game.
